Our Story

Redefining laundry services with extra care, innovative technology, and a vision for excellence

About WOSHO

WOSHO was born from a simple idea - to transform the mundane chore of laundry into a premium, hassle-free experience. Founded in 2025 by two visionary students from IIIT Sonipat.

Our journey began in the dorm rooms of IIIT Sonipat, where our founders Nitin Shukla and Arpit Balyian recognized the need for a laundry service that treated clothes with the care they deserve. What started as a college project quickly evolved into a full-fledged premium service.

Today, WOSHO stands as a testament to innovation in the laundry industry, serving discerning clients who appreciate the difference quality care makes to their garments.

This platform will connect users with verified local laundry partners, offering both pickup and on-site service options. Customers can easily search for nearby laundry providers through the app or website, schedule pickups at their convenience, or request home service for immediate assistance. This model aims to bring convenience, transparency, and speed to everyday laundry needs. By providing real-time tracking, service ratings, and flexible payment options, the platform ensures a smooth and trustworthy experience for both users and laundry partners.

Our Mission

"To redefine laundry care by making it instant, reliable, and effortless—so you never have to choose between clean clothes and your precious time. We empower local laundry partners and professionals to deliver quality service at your doorstep, blending technology with trust to simplify everyday life."

Our Vision

"To become the most trusted household name in laundry care across India – where clean clothes are no longer a chore, but an instant, affordable service delivered with the tap of a button. We envision a future where every neighborhood thrives with empowered local laundry partners, sustainable practices, and seamless technology that saves millions of hours for busy lives."

Our Values

At Wosho, we live by five core principles: putting customers first through effortless service, delivering uncompromising quality in every wash, empowering local businesses with our platform, using technology to build trust, and committing to eco-friendly practices. These values drive us to redefine laundry care - making it faster, fairer and more sustainable for everyone. Clean. Convenient. Conscious. That's the Wosho way.
